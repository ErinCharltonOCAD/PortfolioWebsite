raw materials



0.1

/photography

Interested in how designers can manipulate material value through careful presentation, I created a photography series of household substances removed from their usual context.

Things we usually recognize as play-dough, a bar of soap, paint, clear glue and ramen noodles become intriguing formal subjects when presented through a new visual language.
